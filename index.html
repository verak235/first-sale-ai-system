Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need tech experience?

Not at all. This system is designed for complete beginners. If you can write a text message, you can do this.

How much time do I need each day?

Most steps take 15-30 minutes. You can spread them across the week however works best for your schedule.

What kind of products can I sell?

PDF guides, checklists, templates, short email courses, video tutorials — anything that packages your knowledge into something helpful.

What if I don't know what to sell?

Week 1 walks you through discovering your strengths and choosing a topic. Most moms are surprised by how much they already know.

Is this really free?

Yes. The guide, the workflow, the AI prompts — all free. The tools we recommend (like Gumroad) are also free to start. You only pay platform fees when you make a sale.

What if I don't make a sale in 30 days?

That is perfectly okay. The system is designed so that everything you build — your product, your page, your confidence — stays with you. You can keep going at your own pace.
